Nearby Attractions

Explore Mumbai from Hotel Gateway Star

ISKCON Juhu

Just a short drive from the hotel, ISKCON Juhu is a peaceful spiritual retreat. This iconic temple attracts thousands of visitors every day and is a must-visit for its beautiful architecture and serene ambiance.

Juhu Beach

Relax by the sea at Juhu Beach, one of Mumbai’s most famous beaches. Perfect for evening strolls, street food, and stunning sunsets, it’s a great escape just minutes from our budget-friendly hotel near Mumbai Airport.

Mahakali Caves

Located close to Hotel Gateway Star, Mahakali Caves is a peaceful spot featuring ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves. A great place for a quiet visit, it’s easily accessible from our budget-friendly hotel near Mumbai Airport and one of the best hotels in Andheri East. Ideal for a quick cultural outing during your stay.
