About Us

Welcome to Hotel Gateway Star – Your Comfort Stay Near Mumbai Airport

Hotel Gateway Star is a trusted name for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and affordability. Ideally located near Mumbai International Airport, we are proud to be one of the best hotels near Mumbai airport for both business and leisure stays.

We understand the needs of modern travelers and offer clean, well-maintained rooms, friendly service, and essential amenities – all at budget-friendly prices. Whether you’re on a short layover or a longer visit, our hotel is designed to make you feel at home.

As one of the best hotels in Andheri and a top-rated hotel in Andheri East, we are close to business hubs like Seepz and Andheri Kurla Road, making us the ideal choice for corporate guests. Our location also ensures easy access to shopping, dining, and transport options.
